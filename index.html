About Us

Vision

To be a leading center of excellence in AI and ML education, research, and innovation, producing globally competent professionals who contribute to technological advancement and societal betterment.

Mission

To provide world-class education in artificial intelligence and machine learning, foster cutting-edge research, and develop ethical AI solutions that address real-world challenges.

Objectives

Deliver industry-relevant curriculum, promote research excellence, establish industry partnerships, and nurture innovative thinking among students and faculty.
